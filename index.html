Indian Red Sauce Pasta:

From simple dough to hundreds of shapes, PASTA is the world's favorite canvas for flavor. Affordable, shelf-stable, and perfectly paired with any sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of any shape
  • Tomatoes and Onions
  • cooking oil
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Any cruchy veggies
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta
    • 1 cup pasta in salted water (8–10 mins). Save a splash of water, drain, and set aside.
  2. Saute the veggies
    • Fry: 2 tbsp oil, 1 tsp minced garlic, and 1 chopped onion until soft
    • Toss: Add capsicum and corn; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Make the sauce
    • Simmer: Add 1 cup tomato puree. Cook for 5 minutes.
    • Season: Stir in ½ tsp chili powder, ½ tsp oregano, 2 tbsp ketchup, a pinch of sugar, and salt.
  4. Mix & Serve
    • Toss: Add pasta to the sauce (use saved water if dry). Top with cheese and serve!
